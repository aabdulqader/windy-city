Something About Us

World-Class Sign Studio

Windy City Arts is a sign studio in an environment of world-renowned Businesses, Architects, and Organizations that have defined and molded us into what we are today. We are constantly evolving, setting the local tone for state-of-the-art custom made signage.

Based in Hailey, Idaho our team includes specialists in Project Management, Graphic Design, Production, and Installation.

Customer Service

Professional Installation

We come to you! Our team of installation technicians will come to your location to ensure that the product is installed safely and within the correct specifications.

Industry Workhorses

To ensure quality, we use the best substrates, films, inks, lamination and construction methods available. It is our goal to always improve.

Eco-Friendly

We use safe, recyclable materials and Eco-Sol ink. Limiting material waste and avoiding hard chemicals whenever possible.

Weather Resistant
